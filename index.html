Lobitos & Pedritas

About this website: Targeted towards the locals and the government of Libidos and Piedritas detailing engineering and other general solutions on how to improve the local area. For example, creating an online guideline on how to improve the local fishermans' workplace by showing them how to improve and integrate existing infrastructure so that they can work with tourists could vastly aid in attracting tourism in return giving a better opportunity to have well-founded and long-lasting relationships as being The tourist attraction in north of Peru. This in return, would create a stable income to the local area, overall boosting the economy in the area and ultimately have the foundation to improve other areas of the local area. There is actually no documentation online available to the locals of Libidos and Piedritas highlighting how individuals within the local community could help improve the area. Whilst our website offers bigger projects feasible only for the government/ companies it is also important that the community within these areas too have access to modern engineering especially if that resource is readily available for free and at all times. Whilst accessible electricity for the residents of Peru would be an obvious limitation for having access to this website, book guides could be sent out too
