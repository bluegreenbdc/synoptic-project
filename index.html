Lobitos & Piedritas

This website is intended for the government, the community and the future tourists of Lobitos & Pedritas.

Government - We aim to raise awareness to the government of L&P of the potential fixes to the local area.

Locals - We aim to help the community of L&P directly by creating government approved licensing for fishing and the making of new businesses related to fishing. Additonally once our website is out of beta, our aim is to create a complete set of a engineering guide for locals to learn from and improve their local area whilst benefiting in the long term.

Tourists - We want to show people around the world the beauty of L&P. In this initiative, tourism in L%P is a crucial part of making the local area better for the locals whilst also progressively being an amazing attraction to tourists
